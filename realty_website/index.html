About Burnham

Burnham is an advanced AI communication assistant that steps in when you can't answer calls, handling customer interactions with human-like conversation and intelligence.

How It Works

When a customer calls and you're unavailable, Burnham immediately contacts them with a voice that's indistinguishable from human. It follows intelligent conversation paths, asks preset questions, and adapts based on responses.

Key Capabilities

  • Natural voice conversations that sound completely human
  • Instant follow-up when calls are missed
  • Mass SMS campaigns to 10-20 numbers simultaneously
  • Intelligent response handling based on customer input
  • Automatic contact information capture for CRM integration
  • Customizable conversation flows for different scenarios
